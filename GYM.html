Welcome To Gym Town

Gym Town has been the authority in fitness since 1965 dating back to the original Gym Town in Venice, California. It was the place for serious fitness. Opened long before the modern day health club existed, the original Gym Town featured homemade equipment and a dedication to getting results. It was an instant hit. Gym Town quickly became known as “The Mecca of Bodybuilding.''
